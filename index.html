Organizational Structure

The GSO is led by the General Services Officer and supported by specialized teams handling procurement, property custodianship, maintenance, and logistics. Each unit collaborates to ensure streamlined operations and service delivery.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: Who can request services from GSO?
A: All local government departments and authorized personnel can request services.

Q: How are procurement requests processed?
A: Departments must submit a Purchase Request (PR) form. GSO evaluates, sources, and coordinates the purchase through proper channels.
